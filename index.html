About Us

Interhax's mission is to make hackathons accessible for all youth in India to inspire more talented individuals to pursue careers in the tech industry. What makes our hackathon so unique is that we focus on tailoring our event specifically to the needs of our target audience. Through adjusting times to IST from typical US times, choosing prizes less reliant on a proficient postal service, providing extensive support for beginners to build amazing projects, and hosting all workshops in our attendees' native language of Hindi, we strive to offer the most convenient experience for our attendees and help them kickstart their journey as a programmer.

F.A.Q

  • When will this event be happening?

    The date for this event is currently tentative, but you can expect it to take place in 6 months.

  • Do I need coding experience to attend?

    No, we highly encourage beginners to attend, as our event is actually tailored to those with zero coding experience. we will provide numerous workshops to help you get introduced to vast world of coding.

  • What are the rules to submit a project??

    Hacks must be made by groups of up to four people, no more. Other than pre-made external libraries and tools, the code can only be made during the event and must be submitted before the deadline.

  • What if I don't have any teammates?

    At the start of the event, we will hold a meeting to help pair attendees who are still looking for collaborators to work with. Interhax is a great way to meet new people and connect with them!

  • How do I sign up?

    Sign-ups will be posted soon

  • I have a different question

    Email us at tointerhax@gmail.com. We will answer any question you might have!
